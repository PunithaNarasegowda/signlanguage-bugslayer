How it works?

 Users enable their webcam or device camera on our website to capture video input of their hand gestures while signing.


 Computer vision algorithms analyze the pre-processed video feed in real-time to detect and recognize the hand gestures and movements associated with sign language.


 Hand shape, movement trajectory, and finger positions, are extracted from the video frames.


The classified gestures are then translated into text or spoken language, providing a meaningful interpretation of the sign language input.


 The translated text output is displayed to the user, providing feedback  based on their sign language input.
